Built for Fleet Managers

Recognition that goes beyond the box.

Physical kits make an impact on delivery day. A driver newsletter keeps that recognition alive all quarter — inside the cab, on break room bulletin boards, and shared with family.

The DAS Newsletter Builder lets any fleet manager create a professional, fully branded quarterly publication without a design team. Pick your content modules, add your drivers, and generate a shareable PDF in minutes. Every portal account gets full access — no extra charge.

  • Driver Spotlight sections — name, photo, tenure, and a personal recognition message
  • Safety award callouts — highlight clean safety records and DOT milestones
  • Your brand, your colors — logo, fleet name, and color scheme applied throughout
  • One shareable link — email it, print it, or post it on your internal portal

How It Works

From blank page to published
newsletter in three steps.

1
Set up your fleet profile
Add your logo, fleet colors, and driver roster to the portal once. Every newsletter you build after that inherits your brand automatically — no re-entering information.
2
Choose modules & drivers
Select which content modules to include — Driver Spotlight, Safety Awards, Years of Service, and more. Tag which drivers appear in each section. The wizard walks you through each step.
3
Generate & share
Hit generate. The portal builds a professional PDF using your brand and content in minutes. Share it via a single link — email it, print it, or post it on your bulletin board.
