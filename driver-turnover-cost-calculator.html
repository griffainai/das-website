What goes into replacing a driver.

🔍

Recruiting & advertising

Job boards, recruiter time, referral bonuses, and screening for every open seat.

📝

Orientation & onboarding

Orientation pay, trainer time, paperwork, and ramp before the driver is productive.

🚙

Empty-seat revenue

The freight that truck can't move while the seat sits open — often the biggest hidden cost.

Against these numbers, a branded appreciation gift or a recognition program is a rounding error — and it moves the retention rate this calculator is so sensitive to.
