The Business Case

At $50/driver, commercial driver gifts are the highest-ROI retention investment available.

Replacing a single commercial driver costs $8,000–$15,000 in recruiting, onboarding, training, and lost productivity (ATRI). Most fleet managers know the number but struggle to justify line-item spend on recognition programs when budgets are tight.

The math is straightforward: if a $50 commercial driver gift kit contributes to retaining one driver who would otherwise leave, it pays for the entire program for a 300-driver fleet — and then some. Fleets with structured recognition programs report 20–35% lower voluntary turnover than fleets without them.

The question isn't whether you can afford to run a commercial driver gift program. It's whether you can afford not to.
