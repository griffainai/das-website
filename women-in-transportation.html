Why Signature Purple?

A distinctive color that signals a special initiative — without replacing your brand.

While most recognition programs are produced in standard corporate colors, this collection optionally introduces a Signature Purple palette. It does not replace or diminish your corporate brand — it creates a distinctive recognition experience that immediately identifies the collection as part of a special initiative focused on celebrating women in transportation. Much like organizations use special colors for safety campaigns, community initiatives, or military appreciation, Signature Purple serves as a visual symbol of recognition, appreciation, and professional achievement. Your company logo remains the centerpiece of every item.
