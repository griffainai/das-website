Why it works

A branded gift is the cheapest retention tool a fleet has, and it lands the moment a driver decides whether they are a person here or a truck number.

Replacing one commercial driver runs an estimated 8,000 to 20,000 dollars once recruiting, onboarding, and the empty seat are counted. Against that, an appreciation gift is a rounding error. The fleets that retain best do not gift at random: they run recognition programs where a branded gift arrives on schedule, at the moments that matter.
