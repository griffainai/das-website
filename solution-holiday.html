The Year-End Opportunity

The holidays are the one moment where your driver's family is the audience.

OTR drivers spend weeks away from home during Q4 — the season where everyone else is with their families. They're on I-40 at 3 AM on Thanksgiving weekend while the rest of the country is home. They know it. Their families know it.

When a premium holiday recognition package arrives — branded, personalized, and clearly came from leadership — it travels home with the driver. Their spouse sees it. Their kids see it. The acknowledgment extends beyond the individual. The family, which often questions whether the sacrifices are worth it, sees evidence that the company values what their driver does.

That family-level impact is the retention lever most fleet managers never think to activate. It's also the reason our holiday programs consistently outperform other recognition products on reorder rate and driver-reported satisfaction.
