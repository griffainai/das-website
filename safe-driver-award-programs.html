Safety recognition pays for itself

A single preventable accident can cost a fleet well over six figures once you count claims, downtime, insurance, and reputation. Fleets that formally recognize safe driving see materially fewer incidents — because recognition reinforces the daily habits (pre-trip inspections, defensive driving, accident-free streaks) that prevent them.

Safe Service Miles awards recognize verified accident-free mileage — the higher distinction in driver recognition — and pair naturally with annual Safe Driver Awards that progress year over year.

A program your safety department can run

The annual Safe Driver Award includes a vehicle door decal, lapel pin, and progressive editions (5, 10, 15, 20 years), so each year of safe performance is its own visible milestone. Combine it with safe-mileage medals and recognition kits for a complete safety-incentive program aligned to NSC and DOT safe-driving standards.

Frequently asked questions

What is a safe driver award program?

A structured, recurring way for a fleet to recognize drivers for accident-free performance — typically combining an annual safe-driver award (with progressive year tiers) and safe-mileage medals, all custom-branded to the fleet and aligned to NSC/DOT safe-driving standards.

Do safety incentive programs reduce accidents?

Fleets with formal safety-recognition programs consistently report fewer preventable incidents. Recognition reinforces the daily behaviors — inspections, defensive driving, accident-free streaks — that keep drivers and the public safe, and at $140K+ per preventable accident the ROI is decisive.

Can the program scale across a large fleet?

Yes — we build the recognition calendar with you and handle fulfillment at fleet scale: free shipping, volume pricing for 100+ drivers, Net-30 terms, and delivery to one hub or to individual drivers.
