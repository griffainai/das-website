Questions

Milestone awards, answered.

A million-mile driver award recognizes a professional driver who has driven one million miles safely, typically a decade or more of accident-free driving. DAS offers custom-engraved, fleet-branded awards from 1 million through 6 million miles.

Years-of-service, or tenure, awards honor career milestones, commonly 5, 10, 20, and 30-plus years. They are a separate track from safe-driving recognition and are custom-engraved with the fleet and driver name.

Yes. All milestone awards are custom-engraved with your fleet branding and the driver name. Minimum order is 10 units, with reorders available for individual drivers.

Safe-driving awards recognize accident-free performance, annual or by safe miles; service awards recognize tenure, the years employed. DAS keeps the two tracks separate so a driver safety record and career length are honored distinctly.
