Recognition is the cheapest retention lever you have

The OTR sector runs roughly 89% annual driver turnover, and replacing a single driver costs $8,000–$20,000 once you count recruiting, screening, orientation, and the 60–90 day productivity gap. Against that, a premium, custom-branded appreciation kit is a rounding error — and it works because drivers keep and use it every day.

These aren’t cheap trinkets. Think quality gear (YETI, Stanley, premium tools and wellness items) in a branded presentation case — the difference between something a driver tosses and something they reach for in the cab for years.

A kit for every moment in the driver lifecycle

Welcome new hires with onboarding kits that set the tone from day one. Recognize Driver Appreciation Week, safety milestones, peak-season pushes, and the holidays with kits sized to your fleet. Our Premium Appreciation Kits combine practical tools, comfort, and wellness items into a professional recognition experience — not a giveaway.

Frequently asked questions

Do driver appreciation gifts actually reduce turnover?

Yes — structured recognition is one of the most cost-effective retention levers available. Fleets with formal recognition programs consistently report lower voluntary turnover, and the math is decisive: a recognition program costs a small fraction of the $8,000–$20,000 it takes to replace a driver who leaves.

Can we order in bulk with Net-30 terms?

Yes. We’re built for fleet purchasing — free shipping, volume pricing for 100+ drivers, Net-30 terms (subject to approval), and the option to ship to one hub or to individual driver addresses.

Are the gifts customized to our fleet?

Every kit is custom-branded with your fleet logo, colors, and message. A specialist coordinates the artwork after your order so the kit reflects your operation.
