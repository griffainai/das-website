Questions

Recognition programs, answered.

A repeatable system of branded appreciation moments across onboarding, Driver Appreciation Week, safety milestones, and tenure anniversaries, designed to make drivers feel valued and reduce turnover. It differs from a one-off gift by being scheduled, branded, and tied to specific milestones.

Recognition is one of the strongest low-cost retention levers in fleet operations. Against an 8,000 to 20,000 dollar replacement cost per driver, a structured program typically pays for itself many times over.

It scales with headcount and how many moments you recognize. Per-driver budgets typically run 25 to 200 dollars per moment, with volume pricing and Net-30 for 100 or more drivers.

Start with an onboarding welcome kit for every hire and a fleet-wide Driver Appreciation Week kit each September, then add safety and tenure recognition as you grow.
