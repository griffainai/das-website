Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how DRIVER APPRECIATION SOLUTIONS INC. ("DAS," "we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you visit driverappreciationsolutions.com, purchase from us, or participate in our text-messaging program. By using our website or services, you agree to this Policy. It works alongside our Terms & Conditions and SMS Terms.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly and information collected automatically:

You provide: your name, company/fleet name, email address, mobile phone number, shipping/billing address, and the details of your quote request, order, or inquiry. If you opt in to text messages, we collect your mobile number and your consent record (date, time, and source of opt-in).

Collected automatically: basic device and usage data (pages viewed, referring page, approximate region, and similar analytics) via cookies and standard web technologies, to operate and improve the site.

Payment: card payments are processed by our payment processor (Stripe). We do not store full card numbers on our servers.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to: respond to your quotes, inquiries, and support requests; process and fulfill orders and send order and proof updates; operate our customer-care text-messaging program (below); maintain your account; comply with legal obligations; and improve our website and services. We do not use your information for automated decision-making that produces legal effects.

3. Text Messaging (SMS) Program

Our SMS program is a customer-care program: after you contact us and opt in (by checking the SMS-consent box and submitting your mobile number on a DAS form), we may text you in response to your request — quotes and pricing access, program planning and consultation scheduling, order and proof updates, and answers to your questions. Consent is not a condition of any purchase. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out at any time and HELP for help. Full details are in our SMS Terms & Conditions.

Our commitment to your mobile data: No mobile information will be sold, rented, or shared with any third parties or affiliates for their own marketing or promotional purposes. Text-messaging originator opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third parties. All the above categories exclude text-messaging originator opt-in data and consent information — this information will not be shared with any third parties.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers who help us operate — for example, our payment processor (Stripe), authentication/hosting and database providers (e.g., Firebase, Vercel), our SMS/telephony provider, and email/shipping vendors — and only as needed to provide the service to you. These providers are bound to use the information solely to perform services for us. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights. As stated above, SMS opt-in data and consent are never shared with any third parties, and no mobile information is shared with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Cookies & Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to keep the site working (e.g., your cart), remember preferences, and measure site performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling some cookies may affect site functionality.

6. Data Retention

We keep personal information for as long as needed to provide our services, maintain records of transactions, comply with legal and tax obligations, and resolve disputes. When information is no longer needed, we delete or de-identify it.

7. Your Choices & Rights

You may: opt out of texts at any time by replying STOP; unsubscribe from marketing email using the link in any email; and request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information by emailing us. We will respond consistent with applicable law. You can update account details by signing in to your account.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we work to protect your data and limit access to those who need it to serve you.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website and services are intended for business customers and are not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective Date" above shows when it was last revised. Material changes will be posted on this page; your continued use of our services after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
