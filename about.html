Our Mission

Recognition isn't a perk.
It's a retention strategy.

The OTR sector runs at 89% annual turnover among large carriers. ATRI puts the cost of replacing a single driver at $8,000–$15,000 — before you account for lost productivity, training hours, or the cascading effect on team morale. These aren't abstract statistics. They're line items on every fleet operations budget.

Driver Appreciation Solutions was founded by transportation veterans who understood one thing clearly: most carriers know they need recognition programs. What they lack is a partner who can execute them at fleet scale — with the right products, the right cadence, and the credibility to do it without it feeling like a corporate afterthought.

What We Stand For

Three principles behind
every product we ship.

Built for Trucking. Not Adapted from Gifting.

Every kit originates from the trucking profession — not repurposed from a corporate gifting catalog. The products, language, materials, and packaging reflect the specific culture and standards of commercial drivers. That difference is visible the moment a driver opens the box.

Program Architecture. Not Just Products.

We work with VP Safety and operations teams to structure recognition calendars that align with Driver Appreciation Week, service milestones, onboarding, and seasonal programs. The result is a repeatable system — not a one-time order that gets forgotten until next September.

Fleet-Grade Quality. No Exceptions.

The quality of a recognition kit signals what the carrier thinks of the driver receiving it. We hold every product to a single standard: if we wouldn't be proud to receive it, it doesn't ship. Premium materials, premium presentation — on every order, at every tier.
